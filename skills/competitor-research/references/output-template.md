## Usage notes

### Confidence assignment
- **High**: Official company source, verified press release, recent and primary
- **Medium**: Analyst estimate (Sacra, Contrary), reputable publication, slightly dated
- **Low**: Single unverified source, inferred data, forum/community post

### When data is missing
Always include the dimension with explicit notation:
- "Not available" — Cannot be found publicly
- "Undisclosed" — Company hasn't shared this
- "Requires premium tool" — Data exists but needs paid access
- "Unknown" — May exist but not found in research

### Source citation
Every data point needs a source. If multiple sources, cite the most authoritative. Include access date for time-sensitive data.
