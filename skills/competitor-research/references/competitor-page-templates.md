# Competitor Page Templates — Reference Guide

Templates for generating competitor comparison and alternative pages at scale. Use these formats to capture high-intent competitive search traffic.

---

## The 4 Competitor Page Formats

| Format | URL Pattern | Search Intent | Content Focus |
|--------|-------------|---------------|---------------|
| **Singular Alternative** | `/[competitor]-alternative/` | Switching from competitor | Your solution as the answer |
| **Plural Alternatives** | `/[competitor]-alternatives/` | Exploring options | List including you |
| **You vs Competitor** | `/[your-product]-vs-[competitor]/` | Direct comparison | Your perspective |
| **A vs B** | `/[competitor-a]-vs-[competitor-b]/` | Category research | Neutral comparison |

---

## Format 1: Singular Alternative

**URL pattern:** `/[competitor]-alternative/`
**Target keyword:** `[competitor] alternative`
**Search intent:** User wants to switch FROM competitor

### Page Structure

```markdown
# The [Competitor] alternative for [ICP]

## Hero Section
- **Headline:** "Looking for a [Competitor] alternative?"
- **Subhead:** "[Your product] does [core value] without [competitor pain]"
- **CTA:** "Try [Your product] free" or "See how we compare"

## Why people switch from [Competitor]
- [Pain point 1 — with specific detail]
- [Pain point 2 — with specific detail]
- [Pain point 3 — with specific detail]
*Source: G2 reviews, customer interviews, etc.*

## How [Your product] is different
| What you need | [Competitor] | [Your product] |
|---------------|-------------|----------------|
| [Need 1] | [Limitation] | [Solution] |
| [Need 2] | [Limitation] | [Solution] |
| [Need 3] | [Limitation] | [Solution] |

## What customers say
> "[Quote about switching from competitor]"
— [Name], [Title], [Company]

## Side-by-side comparison
[Detailed feature comparison table]

## Migration from [Competitor]
- How easy is it to switch?
- What data can you import?
- Migration support available?

## FAQ
- Is [Your product] cheaper than [Competitor]?
- Can [Your product] do X that [Competitor] does?
- How long does it take to switch?

## CTA
"Ready to switch from [Competitor]?"
[Primary CTA button]
```

### SEO Elements

**Title tag:** `[Competitor] Alternative | [Your Product] — [Differentiator]`
**Meta description:** `Looking for a [Competitor] alternative? [Your product] offers [key benefit] without [competitor limitation]. See why teams switch.`
**H1:** `The [Competitor] alternative for [ICP]`

### Key Content Requirements
- [ ] Specific competitor pain points (not generic)
- [ ] Side-by-side comparison with real features
- [ ] Migration/switching information
- [ ] Customer quote about switching
- [ ] FAQ addressing common concerns

**URL pattern:** `/[competitor]-alternatives/`
**Target keyword:** `[competitor] alternatives`
**Search intent:** User exploring multiple options

### SEO Elements

**Title tag:** `[Number] Best [Competitor] Alternatives ([Year]) | [Your Product]`

### Key Content Requirements
- [ ] Genuine alternatives (not just your product)
- [ ] Fair assessment of each option
- [ ] Your product positioned naturally, not forced
- [ ] Comparison criteria clear
- [ ] Updated for current year

## Format 4: A vs B (Third-Party Comparison)

**Target keyword:** `[competitor a] vs [competitor b]`
**Search intent:** Category research, not brand-aware

### Page Structure

- **Headline:** "[Competitor A] vs [Competitor B]"

[2-3 paragraphs]

[2-3 paragraphs]

[Detailed feature-by-feature comparison]

## Pricing comparison
[Side-by-side pricing tables]

## Pros and cons

### [Competitor A]
[Pros and cons]

[Pros and cons]

[Review comparison data]

### SEO Elements

**Title tag:** `[Competitor A] vs [Competitor B] ([Year]) | Complete Comparison`

**H1:** `[Competitor A] vs [Competitor B]: Complete comparison`

### Key Content Requirements
- [ ] Genuinely neutral comparison
- [ ] Your product mentioned but not forced
- [ ] Value even if reader doesn't choose you
- [ ] Thorough coverage of both competitors
- [ ] Builds authority in category

## Internal Linking Architecture

```
                    /alternatives/ (Hub)
                          │
         ┌────────────────┼────────────────┐
         │                │                │
   /competitor-a-    /competitor-b-    /competitor-c-
   alternative/      alternatives/     alternatives/
         │                │                │
         └────────────────┼────────────────┘
                          │
                    [Cross-links]
                          │
         ┌────────────────┼────────────────┐
         │                │                │
   /you-vs-         /you-vs-         /competitor-a-
   competitor-a/    competitor-b/    vs-competitor-b/
```

**Linking rules:**
- Every alternative page links to hub
- Alternative pages cross-link to related comparisons
- Vs pages link to both alternative pages
- Hub links to all children
- Footer includes comparison navigation

## Content Quality Standards

### Minimum Requirements
| Element | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| Word count | 1,500+ words |
| Unique content | 60%+ (not just tables) |
| Feature comparisons | 10+ specific features |
| Pricing | Current, with date noted |
| Reviews | G2/Capterra data cited |

### Freshness Requirements
| Content | Review Frequency |
|---------|-----------------|
| Pricing | Monthly |
| Features | Quarterly |
| Reviews | Quarterly |
| Full page | Semi-annually |

### Quality Checklist
- [ ] Competitor represented fairly
- [ ] Claims are sourced
- [ ] Pricing is current
- [ ] Screenshots are recent
- [ ] Migration info accurate
- [ ] FAQ addresses real questions
- [ ] CTA is appropriate to intent
