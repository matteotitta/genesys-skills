## Executive summary

Linear is a San Francisco-based issue tracking and product development platform that has achieved remarkable success challenging Atlassian's Jira dominance. Founded in 2019 by former Airbnb, Coinbase, and Uber designers and engineers, the company raised an $82M Series C in June 2025 at a $1.25B valuation, reaching unicorn status while maintaining profitability since 2021, a rarity in enterprise SaaS.

The company's differentiation centers on speed, design quality, and opinionated workflows that prioritize developer experience over configurability. With only ~70 employees and 2 product managers, Linear has attracted 15,000+ customers including OpenAI, Perplexity, Cursor, Scale AI, Ramp, and Cash App. The company claims 280% profit growth in 2024 and 2000% enterprise ARR growth, with its first Fortune 100 customer signed. CEO Karri Saarinen reports the company has never spent more cash than it raised ("negative lifetime burn").

Linear represents a significant competitive benchmark for developer-focused B2B SaaS: proof that craft, speed, and product-led growth can defeat entrenched enterprise incumbents without massive sales teams or marketing spend.

## Product overview

### Core product
Linear is a purpose-built issue tracking and product development platform designed for high-performance software teams. It emphasizes speed, keyboard-first navigation, and opinionated workflows over configurability.

### Key features
| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Issue tracking | Fast, keyboard-driven issue creation with markdown, labels, priorities, and custom fields |
| Cycles | Time-boxed sprint planning with automatic rollover and burndown charts |
| Projects & Initiatives | Group issues into projects and strategic initiatives for roadmap planning |
| Linear Insights | Real-time analytics and dashboards for engineering metrics |
| Triage Intelligence | AI-powered issue routing, duplicate detection, and assignee suggestions |
| Linear for Agents | First-class support for AI coding agents (Cursor, Codex, Factory) to receive assignments |
| Linear Asks | Workplace service desk for non-engineering requests via Slack |
| Linear MCP | Model Context Protocol integration for Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor |

### Differentiators
- Speed: Proprietary "Sync Engine" makes interactions feel instant, even offline
- Design quality: Minimalist UI praised for "craft" and focus
- Opinionated: Prescriptive workflows reduce configuration overhead
- Agent-first: 25%+ of workspaces use AI agents; 60%+ in enterprise

### Platform/delivery
- **Type**: Web app, Desktop (Mac, Windows, Linux), Mobile (iOS, Android)
- **Tech stack**: React, TypeScript, offline-first Sync Engine architecture

**Source**: [linear.app](https://linear.app), [Linear Features](https://linear.app/features)

## Pricing

### Pricing model
Per-user subscription with monthly or annual billing. Annual billing offers 20% discount.

### Plans
| Plan | Price (Monthly) | Price (Annual) | Includes |
|------|-----------------|----------------|----------|
| Free | $0 | $0 | 2 teams, 250 issues, 10MB uploads, unlimited members |
| Basic | $10/user/mo | $8/user/mo | 5 teams, unlimited issues, admin roles |
| Business | $15/user/mo | $12/user/mo | Unlimited teams, Triage Intelligence, Insights, SLAs, Asks |
| Enterprise | Custom | Annual only | SAML/SCIM, advanced security, dedicated support, sub-initiatives |

### Notes
- Recent pricing update: Business tier now at $16/user/mo annual per Linear pricing page
- Enterprise pricing requires custom quote
- Startup program: up to 6 months free on Basic/Business
- Non-profits: 75% discount
- Educational: discounted rates available

**Source**: [Linear Pricing](https://linear.app/pricing), [Bardeen](https://www.bardeen.ai/answers/how-much-does-linear-app-cost) | **Confidence**: High

## Go-to-market analysis

### Sales motion
Product-Led Growth (PLG) with emerging enterprise sales motion. Bottom-up adoption from developers who then expand to full organizations.

### Primary channels
- Product virality (team invitations)
- Word of mouth from satisfied developers
- Founder thought leadership (Karri Saarinen)
- Tech press coverage
- Customer case studies (OpenAI, etc.)

### Website messaging themes
- "Purpose-built for planning and building products"
- "Streamline issues, projects, and product roadmaps"
- "Made for modern product teams"
- Speed and performance emphasis

### Primary CTA
"Start building" — Free signup, no credit card required

### Founder/leadership visibility
CEO Karri Saarinen is highly visible on Twitter/X (69K+ followers) and speaks at conferences. Known for design background (Airbnb Design Systems, Coinbase founding designer). Advocates for "craft" and quality in product development. Co-founders Jori Lallo (CPO) and Tuomas Artman (CTO) maintain lower profiles.
