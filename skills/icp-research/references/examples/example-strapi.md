- **Research date**: December 27, 2025

---

## Executive summary

Strapi is the leading open-source headless CMS with 70.8K GitHub stars, $47M in funding (Series B led by CRV), and a community of 150,000+ developers. The platform enables developers to design REST and GraphQL APIs instantly while empowering content teams to manage, publish, and translate content across any frontend framework. Key differentiators include open-source flexibility (MIT license), SOC 2 certification, GDPR compliance, and deployment flexibility via Strapi Cloud (PaaS) or self-hosted Enterprise Edition.

The research identified four distinct ICP segments: Enterprise digital transformation teams (consolidating legacy CMS platforms), high-growth tech companies (developer-first API-first architectures), media and publishing organizations (high-volume multi-channel content delivery), and digital agencies (building scalable client solutions). Named customers include Tesco, Toyota, IBM, NASA, Walmart, Amazon, Prudential, Cisco, L'Équipe, Finary, and Airbus, demonstrating traction across retail, financial services, aerospace, media, and fintech verticals.

TAM opportunity is significant given the headless CMS market's growth trajectory. Strapi's freemium model (open-source base with paid Cloud starting at $15/project and Enterprise at $99/seat/month) enables broad developer adoption with enterprise upsell. G2 reviews indicate 4.5/5 rating with 196 reviews, strong ease of use (9.1/10), robust API/integrations (8.6/10), and content authoring (8.6/10), positioning Strapi as the #1 open-source headless CMS option.

## TAM analysis

### Methodology

Bottom-up estimate based on customer segment analysis combined with headless CMS market sizing and Strapi's funding trajectory ($47M raised, Series B in 2022).

### TAM calculation

| Metric | Value | Source/Basis |
|--------|-------|--------------|
| Total addressable market | $3.8B | Headless CMS market estimate (2025), growing 22% CAGR |
| Serviceable addressable market | $1.4B | Companies with developer teams needing flexible, API-first CMS |
| Serviceable obtainable market | $180M | Open-source adopters + Enterprise/Cloud conversions based on 150K+ community |

### Assumptions

- Enterprise tier pricing at $99/seat/month (billed annually) per G2 and third-party sources.

- 70.8K GitHub stars and 150K+ community members indicate massive developer adoption funnel.

## Negative ICP (anti-personas)

### Who Strapi is NOT for

| Anti-segment | Why poor fit | Warning signals | Evidence |
|--------------|--------------|-----------------|----------|
| Non-technical small businesses | Requires developer for setup; no visual builder like Wix/Squarespace | "I just want to drag and drop," no developer on team | G2 comparisons, product positioning |
| WordPress loyalists | Different paradigm (headless vs monolithic); requires frontend development | "We need themes and plugins like WordPress" | G2 reviews mentioning learning curve |
| Teams wanting all-in-one SaaS | Self-hosted requires infrastructure; even Cloud needs frontend work | "We want everything managed including frontend" | Product architecture |
| Extremely price-sensitive orgs | Enterprise features require paid tiers; infrastructure costs for self-hosted | Budget <$5K/year for CMS, no dev resources | Pricing structure |
| Heavy visual editing needs | Strapi focuses on structured content; limited WYSIWYG compared to Storyblok | "Editors need to see exactly how pages look" | G2 feature comparisons (visual editor 7.2/10) |

### Disqualification criteria

| Signal | Action | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| No developers on team | Disqualify or partner with agency | Strapi requires technical implementation |
| WordPress-first mindset | Qualify carefully; may need education | Different mental model for headless |
| Budget under $10K/year total | Route to Community Edition only | Cloud/Enterprise economics don't work |
| Need for built-in frontend | Suggest alternatives (Webflow, Storyblok) | Headless = BYOF (Bring Your Own Frontend) |
| Heavy regulation without IT | Requires security team for self-hosted | Compliance complexity |
